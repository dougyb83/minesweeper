How to Play

  • You must uncover all the tiles without hitting a mine - Otherwise, it's GAME OVER!
  • Tiles will have either a Mine, a Hint, or a Blank space underneath!
  • If you reveal a blank space, all the surrounding blanks will also be revealed!
  • If you reveal a hint, that number will indicate exactly how many mines are in the 8 surrounding tiles!
  • Place flags where you think there is a mine (these are just for your reference)!
  • Long PressRight Click on the tile to add or remove a flag!
